Pricing

Pricing is kept flexible so the work matches what the business actually needs.

How pricing works

  • Small setup jobs: simple OpenClaw setup, light configuration, or one focused automation need.
  • Mid-size projects: setup plus workflow shaping, enquiry handling, or knowledge support.
  • Custom work: broader business support where setup, guidance, and practical implementation all matter.

What affects price

  • how much setup is needed
  • whether the work is local-only or remote-friendly
  • how much workflow shaping or custom support is involved
  • whether the business needs ongoing help after setup

Best approach: start with a quote based on the simplest useful version of the job.
